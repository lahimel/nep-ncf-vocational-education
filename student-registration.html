Import Students from Excel
Copy your student rows from Excel and paste below — it's fast and easy!

📌 How to paste from Excel:

1
Open your student Excel file (Grade_6, Grade_7 etc. sheet)
2
Select the data rows below the header (from Sr No to Grade column)
3
Press Ctrl+C to copy
4
Select the Grade below, click in the text area, press Ctrl+V to paste
5
Click "Preview & Import" to save!
✅ Columns expected: Sr No | First Name | Middle Name | Last Name | Full Name | Gender | Mother Name | Father Name | Date of Birth | Grade
