Our Approach

NEP - NCF Aligned Vocational Education Implementation

In line with the National Education Policy 2020, National Curriculum Framework 2023, and the State Curriculum Framework 2024, our school implements vocational education to support holistic development. The program emphasizes hands-on learning, practical skills, and real-world exposure, enabling students to link academic learning with career readiness and future opportunities.
