Our Approach

NEP – NCF Aligned
Vocational Education

In line with the National Education Policy 2020, National Curriculum Framework 2023, and the State Curriculum Framework 2024, our programme implements vocational education to support holistic development. The programme emphasises hands-on learning, practical skills, and real-world exposure, enabling students to link academic learning with career readiness and future opportunities.
